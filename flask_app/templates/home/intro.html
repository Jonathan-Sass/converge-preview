Converge

Converge with your potential. Converge with your dreams.

What are we doing here?

Let us start off by saying the following:

  • Converge is NOT here to push hustle culture, or adding unnecessary tasks to your busy life.
  • Converge IS about crystallizing your vision for life, and working with the human system to create sustainable progress toward realizing that vision.

Converge is built to be a coach and facilitator with three primary objectives.

  1. Reflecting deeply about your desires and goals in life.
  2. Breaking down your goals into actionable items, and tracking your progress.
  3. Suggesting and implementing neuroscience and literature backed lifestyle practices to help you fel your best and enjoy the process of working toward your goals.

As the Converge community grows, we would also like Converge to serve as a community building, networking tool to connect you with people of similar goals and interests, and if the community grows enough, maybe, just maybe help build deep, meaningful real life connections with people, for those that are seeking more.


Converge is built around principles like Growth Mindset, Self-Compassion, and the Japanese concept of Kaizen, loosely translated as change for good or continuous improvement. Converge is also about embracing authenticity, recognizing our strengths and weaknesses and working with these traits to improve your quality of life and achieve your dreams.

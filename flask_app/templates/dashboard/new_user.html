Welcome to Converge!

Our mission with Converge is to provide structure for you to get really clear on how you would like your life to look, and create a vision for getting there.

  1. Personalized Routines: We'll suggest science-backed routines and practices to help you feel and function your best.
  2. Achievable Steps: Break down your goals into microtasks and visualize your progress.
  3. Motivation & Progress: Track your journey, stay inspired, and turn your dreams into reality.

A note from our developers...

Converge is in its infancy.

We are working toward providing a feature-rich, engaging experience. Please be patient as we are developing our vision.

We welcome any feedback, whether it be a desired feature, or unexpected behavior and bugs.

If you are reporting a bug, please share a screenshot of the error, and if possible, a description of what you were doing when the error occured.

Please send feedback to: info@convergeapp.com

And another note about privacy and integrity.

You will hopefully notice that although we provide updates and reminders via notifications, our mission is actually to avoid dominating your attention with likes and pleas for constant engagement. We are simply here to share skills and strategies while providing accountability as you work toward your dreams.

In much the same way, we take your privacy seriously and will never sell any of the information you share here to another entity.
